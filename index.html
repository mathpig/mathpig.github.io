My Games and Resources

Pig Game

A platformer in which you try to reach the carrot to beat each level. Dodge lava and wolves. Can play single-player or multiplayer.

Controls:

  • Arrow keys to move.
  • S to create a text version of the current level.
  • P to hide/show pallette for placing blocks.

Pig-pong game

A simple ping-pong game in which the speed of the ball and the computer's paddle increases upon each victory.

Controls:

  • Up and down arrow keys to move the paddle.

Pig-rogue game

A dungeon game similar to Gauntlet. Shoot fireballs at wolves and graves, which spawn wolves. Use potions to destroy nearby enemies. Carrots give keys to unlock doors. Cheese heals health points.

Controls:

Player 1:

  • Left shift to spawn pig or fire bullets.
  • WASD to move.
  • Q to use a potion.

Player 2:

  • Right shift to spawn pig or fire bullets.
  • Arrow keys to move.
  • P to use a potion.

Player 3:

  • N to spawn pig or fire bullets.
  • J, L, I, and K to move left, right, up, and down respectively.
  • M to use a potion.

Player 4:

  • C to spawn pig or fire bullets.
  • F, H, T, and G to move left, right, up, and down respectively.
  • V to use a potion.

Pig-Tanks game

A wild two-player tank game in which each player tries to spray each other with either regular bullets or special super bombs.

Controls:

Player 1:

  • A and D to move left and right.
  • C to fire regular bullets.
  • 1 to fire a super bomb.

Player 2:

  • Left and right arrow keys to move left and right.
  • Forward slash to fire regular bullets.
  • 0 to fire a super bomb.

Piglantis game

A game similar to Atlantis in which you control three pig towers that shoot at the enemy pig ships.

Controls:

  • Tower 1 shoot: Left arrow key.
  • Tower 2 shoot: Up arrow key.
  • Tower 3 shoot: Right arrow key.

Hexpig game

A three-dimensional game similar to Minecraft, but with hexagonal tiles. Uses 2D and 3D Perlin noise to make the block textures and the world layout.

Controls:

  • Click on the screen to go fullscreen.
  • W, S, A, D, Q, and E to move forwards, backwards, turn left, turn right, move up, and move down respectively.
  • Click to mine a highlited block.
  • G, R, L, and B to place grass, rock, lava, and bedrock respecitvely.
  • T to trigger a TNT explosion centered at the focused block. Bedrock is indestructible.

Special tit-for-tat algorithm to play against

Once again, the title says it all. Customize the percentage of a misperception and the number of turns, and then click passive or agressive for each turn.

Quizzing program for memorization

Enter the text you want to memorize and the percent chance that a word will be blanked out. Fill out the form to check how well you memorized the text. Click the "Submit" button to submit. Correct answers will be highlited in green, while incorrect ones will be highlited in red.
